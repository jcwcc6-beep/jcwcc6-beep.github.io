Projects

This is a documentary I did about my mother and her cancer. It is meant to be a testament to other families and highlight our experience
and how we got through this hard time as a family and how our faith brought us closer. I spent about three months filming and editing but
this was my first ever short-film so to say, coming out to 23 minutes long. This was a project I had wanted to do for a while, I just didn’t have
the opportunity until a production course I took last school year and I seized it to the best of my ability.
This is the pilot episode of a docu-series that I have been filming with a professor of mine about music education and student teaching.
Each episode entails a conversation about students and their experience taking music-ed courses or former students and their experiences
getting to where they are now as teachers. The series is directed to be an informative series to help especially kids going into music education
to know what they’re getting into. Personally, I have learned a lot about music-ed and all that the degree entails but even beyond the degree,
how much work it is to get into the teaching field.
This was meant for a project for class but it has become one of my favorite projects I’ve done. It is essentially just a montage of photo and video
from a retreat I shot for but all of the footage you see is mine and it was meant to relay a somber, reflective feeling. I meant for that intentionally
because as I was making it, I was doing a lot of reflecting myself about why I love being behind the camera and why I do the work I do to produce
the best product I can.
