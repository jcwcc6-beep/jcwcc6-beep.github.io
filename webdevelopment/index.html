Welcome to My Portfolio!

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Joseph Winterbower and I am an aspiring photographer and videographer, trying to capture and preserve God's beauty one frame at a time!

Purpose

The purpose of this portfolio is to get my name out there to capture God's glory and beauty.
I love to take photos of landscapes but part of his beauty is personal memories too! I love taking photo and video in moments of worship.
Capturing memories and using them as a method of glorifying God is ultimately my goal and my purpose.
I find great peace and solitude behind a camera and I love bringing a smile to people and preserving memories for them.
